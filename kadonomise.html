Kado no Mise




Kado no Mise, translating to “Corner
Restaurant,” serves Edomae sushi
and simple, delicate Japanese fare,
true to the tastes and form that Chef
Furukawa learned when he first
started cooking professionally in his
hometown of Tokyo.

Wednesday - Sunday | 5:30PM+

Explore Kado no Mise



Kaiseki Furukawa




Kaiseki is a form of Japanese
cooking that draws its philosophy
from tea ceremony traditions in
16th-century Kyoto, Japan. At its
core is the belief that we are at our
best when we experience every
moment with one another as if it
were a once-in-a-lifetime gathering.

Tuesday | 5:30PM & 8PM



Explore Kaiseki Furukawa


Gori Gori Peku



Gori Gori Peku, located across the
hallway from our second floor
dining room and chef's counter, is
an intimate Japanese whisky bar,
housing a comprehensive selection
of premium Japanese whisky.

Every day | 5PM+
